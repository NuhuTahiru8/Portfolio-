Why Join ELXPROLab?

01 - Master High-Performance Application Development

Dive into the world of Elixir to learn the secrets behind crafting scalable and high-quality software. From architecting robust GraphQL setups to harnessing the power of Kafka for efficient message transmission.

Elevate your skills in managing vast datasets with precision and speed. ELXPRO Lab covers advanced Elixir techniques, including Postgres seeding, Kafka consumers, and MongoDB integration.
